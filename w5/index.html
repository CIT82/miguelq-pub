Memorable Moments in Bulldog History
Fresno State vs Washington State
The Bulldog Marching perform their pregame show in the Jimmy Kimmel Los Angeles Bowl at Sofi Stadium. The dogs went on to defeat the Washington Cougars. December 17, 2022
Final score: Fresno State 29 - Washington State 6
Fresno State vs San Diego State
The dogs complete a miracle comeback to defeat the Aztecs and win the oil can trophy. October 29, 2022
Final score: Fresno State 32 - San Diego State 28
Bulldog Legend Jerry Tarkanian gets his jersey retired at Save Mart Center. March 1, 2014
The Fresno State Bulldog Marching band performs their pre-game show at the 2013 Las Vegas Bowl. The Dogs lost against the USC Trojans but that's ok. Fun was had by all.
