Miguel's Rock N Roll Museum

Welcome to the Mike's Rock N Roll Museum Website. The reason for this website is because, the real-life Rock N Roll Hall of Fame is not only really for rock bands. They let any type of artist in from any genre. While that might be good for all artists, they should change the name of that organization to the Music Hall of Fame. I decided to create my own Rock N Roll Hall of Fame where only rock bands are allowed to get in and it features bands that I feel have made a huge impact on the rock community. On this website, you will be able to learn about some rock bands that have inspired me and have been around for decades as well as some new up-and-coming bands which have already made their mark at a young age. Bands such as Greta Van Fleet, Mammoth WVH and The Warning lead the pack of new young rock bands. Proving once and for all that rock is truly not dead.
